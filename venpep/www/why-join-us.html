Why Join Us

Statement of Intent

The Management of VenPep Solutions Private Limited is committed to ensuring that the recruitment and selection of all employees will be fair, open and transparent and will comply with all relevant legislations. Personal information received is dealt with strictest confidence. Inclusion forms an important aspect of the recruitment of staff within the service. Applicants will not be excluded from being considered for a position based specifically on their need, background, culture, religion, gender or economic circumstances. Positions will be offered based on competency, qualification and enthusiasm for the position.

Human Capital Philosophy

VenPep's human capital philosophy is all about deploying empowered leadership across the organization. We call our human capital as "crebels" i.e. creative rebels". We need "a creative" to build a $ billion business & "a rebel" to challenge status quo, constantly.


VenPep believes in empowering its human capital to un-leash their un-limited energy and to harness their full potential. VenPep promotes culture of "Learning - Un-learning - Re-learning". Prime focus is to enable our teams to ideate and in the bargain, even if honest mistakes are committed, search thy soul, learn lesson and move ahead only to succeed. We also tend to infuse fresh energy by effective role rotation and allowing open inter-department interface by dis-mantling silos. Last but not the least, VenPep have a policy of respect for women & decent gender mix in the team.


VenPep intends to be a "Leadership Factory" and "Entrepreneurs' Den" for its human capital. Come and be a part of the "crebels' community".

Human Capital Identification Acquisition & Development Process (HCIAD Process)

VenPep shall identify & acquire people

  • who perceive themselves as entrepreneurs - passionate to create & build something from scratch
  • who are hungry for name, fame & growth that is long term
  • who we identify having long term horizon and ability to expand their circle of influence
  • who will roll up sleeves and ready for quick moves, if desirable in the fast changing environment
  • who are natural innovators
  • who have impeccable integrity
  • who are crebels

VenPep shall provide one of the best environments for human capital development that provides

  • robust induction
  • on the job learning & training
  • encouragement to people with appetite for ideation - doing things differently & doing different things
  • Customer Delightopen & un-biased performance & skills assessment
  • gap identification & development
  • job rotation as per skills and learning acumen
  • multi-directional growth
  • VenPep team member shall create his/her own destiny

VenPep thrives to check the appetite for quality professionals. Overall, VenPep needs, "Learners & Leaders".
